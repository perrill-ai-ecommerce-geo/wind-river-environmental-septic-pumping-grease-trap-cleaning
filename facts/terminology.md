# Terminology — WRE services

## Septic pumping
Removing accumulated solids and liquids from a septic tank to help maintain system function.

## Grease trap (and grease interceptor)
Devices used to capture fats, oils, and grease (FOG) before they enter plumbing and sewer lines.
(Note: usage varies; “grease interceptor” often refers to larger external units.)

## FOG
Fats, oils, and grease (common in food service operations).

## Wastewater management
A broad category that can include septic, grease, drain, treatment, hauling, and related services depending on customer type and location.
