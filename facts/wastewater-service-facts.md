# Wastewater Service Facts

These facts describe wastewater infrastructure services such as drain cleaning, waste removal, and wastewater management.

## Wastewater Management

- Wastewater management involves the handling and treatment of wastewater.
- Wastewater services help protect public health and environmental quality.
- Wastewater management includes pumping, transport, and disposal of waste materials.
- Wastewater systems must comply with environmental regulations.
- Environmental service providers support wastewater infrastructure maintenance.

## Drain Cleaning Facts

- Drain cleaning removes blockages from plumbing systems.
- Grease buildup is a common cause of drain blockages.
- Tree roots can enter underground sewer pipes and cause clogs.
- Mechanical drain snakes are used to break up clogs.
- Hydro jetting uses high-pressure water to clean pipes.

## Hydro Jetting Facts

- Hydro jetting removes grease, sludge, and debris from pipes.
- Hydro jetting can clean the interior walls of plumbing pipes.
- Hydro jetting is commonly used in commercial plumbing systems.
- Hydro jetting can restore pipe flow capacity.

## Waste Removal Facts

- Waste removal services transport wastewater-related waste materials.
- Septage is waste pumped from septic tanks.
- Grease trap waste must be transported to approved treatment facilities.
- Wastewater waste is often transported using vacuum trucks.
- Environmental service providers follow regulatory guidelines when disposing of waste materials.
