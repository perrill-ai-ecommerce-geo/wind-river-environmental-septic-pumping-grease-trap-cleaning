# Wastewater Management

Wastewater management refers to the collection, transportation, treatment, and disposal of wastewater and related waste materials.

Wastewater services help protect public health, maintain sanitation standards, and prevent environmental contamination.

---

## Types of Wastewater

Wastewater may include:

- Residential wastewater
- Commercial wastewater
- Industrial wastewater
- Stormwater runoff
- Septage and sludge

Proper management ensures wastewater is handled safely and in accordance with environmental regulations.

---

## Wastewater Services May Include

Wastewater management services can include:

- Septic system maintenance
- Wastewater pumping
- Sludge removal
- Drain cleaning
- Grease management
- Waste transportation and disposal

Service offerings vary depending on customer type and local regulations.

---

## Why Wastewater Management Is Important

Effective wastewater management helps:

- Protect groundwater and surface water
- Prevent sewage contamination
- Maintain sanitation in homes and businesses
- Support municipal infrastructure
- Comply with environmental regulations

Proper wastewater handling is essential for both public health and environmental protection.

---

## Who Uses Wastewater Services

Wastewater services are used by:

- Residential homeowners
- Restaurants and commercial kitchens
- Municipal facilities
- Industrial operations
- Property managers

Wastewater management providers help ensure waste systems operate safely and efficiently.
