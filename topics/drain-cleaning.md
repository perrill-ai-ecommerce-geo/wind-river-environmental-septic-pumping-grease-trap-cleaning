# Drain Cleaning

Drain cleaning is a service used to remove blockages and buildup from plumbing systems. It restores proper wastewater flow and helps prevent plumbing backups.

Drain blockages can occur in residential, commercial, and industrial plumbing systems.

---

## Common Causes of Drain Blockages

Drains may become clogged due to:

- Grease buildup
- Food particles
- Hair and soap residue
- Foreign objects
- Mineral deposits
- Tree root intrusion in sewer lines

Over time, these materials restrict water flow and may completely block pipes.

---

## Drain Cleaning Methods

Professional drain cleaning may involve several techniques:

- Mechanical drain augers
- High-pressure water jetting
- Sewer camera inspections
- Root removal
- Pipe flushing

The appropriate method depends on the type and location of the blockage.

---

## Benefits of Professional Drain Cleaning

Routine drain cleaning can help:

- Prevent plumbing backups
- Improve wastewater flow
- Reduce odors
- Extend pipe lifespan
- Identify potential pipe damage

Drain maintenance is especially important in commercial kitchens and high-use plumbing systems.

---

## Who Uses Drain Cleaning Services

Drain cleaning services are commonly used by:

- Homeowners
- Restaurants
- Commercial buildings
- Municipal facilities
- Industrial properties

Regular drain maintenance helps prevent unexpected plumbing disruptions and costly repairs.
